LA LIGA BUCKS SCORING SYSTEM

Each team earns La Liga Bucks in three categories each week:

  • ESPN COMPONENT (1-12 POINTS): Based on ESPN league standings (1st = 12 points, 12th = 1 point)
  • CUMULATIVE COMPONENT (1-12 POINTS): Based on total season points ranking
  • WEEKLY AVERAGE COMPONENT (1-12 POINTS): Based on weekly scoring average ranking

MAXIMUM WEEKLY TOTAL: 36 LA LIGA BUCKS

Season totals determine playoff seeding and championship eligibility.

MONEY DISTRIBUTION

Weekly high score bonus: $50

Championship and playoff payouts determined at season end.
