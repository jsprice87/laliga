  • TEAMS: 12 teams compete in La Liga del Fuego
  • SEASON: 17 weeks (14 regular season + 3 playoff weeks)
  • PLATFORM: ESPN Fantasy Football
  • DRAFT: Snake draft format with randomized order
  • SCHEDULE: Regular season weeks 1-14, playoffs weeks 15-17
LA LIGA BUCKS SCORING SYSTEM

Each team earns La Liga Bucks in three categories every week:

üèÜ ESPN COMPONENT (1-12 POINTS)

Based on your current ESPN league standings:

  • 1st place = 12 La Liga Bucks
  • 2nd place = 11 La Liga Bucks
  • 3rd place = 10 La Liga Bucks
  • ... and so on ...
  • 12th place = 1 La Liga Buck

üìà CUMULATIVE COMPONENT (1-12 POINTS)

Based on your total season points ranking:

  • Highest total points = 12 La Liga Bucks
  • 2nd highest total points = 11 La Liga Bucks
  • ... and so on ...
  • Lowest total points = 1 La Liga Buck

‚ö° WEEKLY AVERAGE COMPONENT (1-12 POINTS)

  • 2nd highest weekly average = 11 La Liga Bucks
  • ... and so on ...
  • Lowest weekly average = 1 La Liga Buck
MAXIMUM WEEKLY TOTAL: 36 LA LIGA BUCKS
PLAYOFF SYSTEM

üèÜ CHAMPIONSHIP BRACKET (Top 6 Teams)

  • Seeds 1-2: Bye week, advance directly to semifinals
  • Week 15 Wild Card: #3 vs #6, #4 vs #5
  • Week 16 Semifinals: #1 vs Wild Card winner, #2 vs Wild Card winner
  • Week 17 Championship: Semifinal winners compete for the title

üíÄ SACKO BRACKET (Bottom 6 Teams)

  • Seeds 11-12: "Bye" week (safe from Sacko for one week)
  • Week 15 Wild Card: #7 vs #8, #9 vs #10
  • Week 16 Semifinals: #11 vs Wild Card winner, #12 vs Wild Card winner
  • Week 17 Sacko Bowl: Semifinal winners compete to avoid the Sacko
  • LOSER of Sacko Bowl = 2025 SACKO

SEEDING: Playoff seeding is determined by total La Liga Bucks at the end of week 14.

TIEBREAKERS

üéØ LA LIGA BUCKS TIEBREAKERS

When teams are tied in La Liga Bucks, the following tiebreakers apply in order:

  1. Total Points For: Higher total season points wins
  2. Head-to-Head Record: Better record in games between tied teams
  3. Division Record: Better record within division (if applicable)
  4. Total Points Against: Lower total points against wins
  5. Coin Flip: Final tiebreaker determined by commissioner

üèà ESPN PLAYOFF TIEBREAKERS

For ESPN playoff seeding (separate from La Liga Bucks):

  1. Overall Record (wins-losses)
  2. Total Points For
  3. Head-to-Head Record
  4. Total Points Against
